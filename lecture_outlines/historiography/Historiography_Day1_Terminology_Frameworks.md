---

## Slide 12: Rational Human Model

### Slide content
- Assumes most people set rational goals for themselves and seek to achieve them through the exercise of reason and logic.
- Engine of Change is Human Agency
- Attacked by historicist theories, such as Marxism.


---

## Slide 13: Determinism

### Slide content
- A theory that history is shaped primarily by forces **beyond individual human agency**
- Human choices matter, but are constrained by deeper forces
- Examples:
  - Karl Marx — material conditions / class conflict
  - Sigmund Freud — unconscious drives / repression shaping culture




---

## Slide 14: Great Man Theory

### Slide content
- Assumes that every so often, a great* man will arise and alter the course of history.
- Form of Rational Human Model and method of “doing” history
- *Great meaning significant or influential and not necessarily “good”


---

## Slide 15: Classroom Discussion

### Slide content
- Critical Thinking Exercise:
- Consider alternatives to the Great Man Theory, i.e., what else might be an engine of change in history.
