## Slide 23: Nationalism

### Slide content
- Loyalty and devotion to a nation-state; especially: a sense of national consciousness exalting one’s nation above all others.
- One of the most powerful forces in modern history
- Mistakenly thought to be a permanent factor in political behavior
- Prior to the 18th century, **modern nationalism** and the **modern nation-state** were not fully developed
- Many pre-modern empires claimed (or imagined) **universal legitimacy**


### Speaker notes
